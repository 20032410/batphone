0.92-RC3
* MeshMS text messages display delivery confirmations and timestamps, and only stored encrypted, but are not backwards compatible
* Vastly simplified the connection screen
* Added phone dialler for tablet users
* Our routing protocol has been improved
* The Peer List is more responsive to changing network conditions
* Reduced power consumption in a number of use cases

For a complete list of changes, see https://github.com/servalproject/batphone/blob/release-0.92/CURRENT-RELEASE.md
